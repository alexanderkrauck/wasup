Privacy Policy — Wasup (Linz Event Index)

Controller: Alexander Krauck, Linz, Austria — alexander.krauck@gmail.com

What we process

Publicly published event information (titles, dates, venues, descriptions, prices, links) collected from public websites. Organizer names appear only as the source published them. We apply suppression heuristics: events that look private (residential address, personal organizer) are published without location data.

What we do NOT process

No user accounts, no tracking, no cookies, no profiling of API or website users. API access logs (IP address, timestamp, endpoint) are kept short-term for abuse prevention only.

AI assistants (ChatGPT, Claude, …)

When you use Wasup through an AI assistant, your questions are handled by that assistant under its own privacy policy. Wasup only receives the resulting search filters (e.g. date range, category) and returns public event data. We do not receive your identity or conversation.

Inferred attributes

Audience estimates (e.g. expected age range) are clearly labeled machine estimates with confidence values — never assertions about individuals.

Storage & location

EU-hosted (Germany/Austria). Event claims are kept as an append-only history for data-quality purposes.

Your rights (GDPR)

Organizers can request correction or removal of their data (takedown) at the address above; deletion requests are honored including in the claims history via redaction. Response within 48 hours. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Austrian data protection authority (dsb.gv.at).
